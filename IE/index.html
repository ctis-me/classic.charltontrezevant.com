A friendly message to Internet Explorer users.


So, you think you can come to my website and use Internet Explorer, huh?

Well, that's a bad decision you've made, friend. Especially if that decision was conscious.

Why subject yourself to all of this constant torture? The archaic, stone-age interface?

The constant exploitation of security holes, which flood your beloved machine with viruses galore?

How many times have you gotten a virus?

How many times have you sat patiently, for over 30 seconds, waiting for a web page to load?

How many toolbars that you never wanted are eclipsing this page right now?

Do you find browsing the web a pain?

When using Internet Explorer, all of the above are true.

I used to be like you, too. Then, I got so tired of it, I forced myself to see the light. Now, I'm spreading the gospel.

Internet Explorer is a browser that crushes the dreams of developers and users alike, and compared to browsers such as Google Chrome and Mozilla Firefox, it appears more as a neglected, cold, unforgiving utility than a powerful, elegant deliverer of the great variety of rich content that is so abundant on the modern web.

It's and I think that it's time for you to use a secure, modern browser. Trust me, you'll love it.

Here are some links to powerful, secure, and extremely fast browsers that will never give you another headache again:

Google Chrome "Chrome is a fast, simple, and secure web browser, built for the modern web."

Mozilla Firefox "The free, non-profit browser for desktop and mobile"

Check them out, see what suits you, give one of them a try.

And, who knows, maybe one day when IE isn't so horrible, I can finally take this page down.

You won't regret it.
